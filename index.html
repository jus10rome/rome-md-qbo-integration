Reconnection Information

Last updated: May 17, 2026

This integration is an internal, single-user tool operated by Rome MD Consulting PC for its own automated bookkeeping. There is no end-user re-authentication flow because there are no end users other than the company's sole administrator.

When OAuth tokens for this integration expire or need to be re-issued, the system administrator runs the bootstrap script directly on the host server to perform the OAuth consent flow and refresh credentials. This process is handled out-of-band by the administrator and does not require any action from QuickBooks Online customers.

For questions about this integration or its reconnection process, contact: rome.justin@gmail.com

Privacy Policy

Effective May 17, 2026

This integration accesses QuickBooks Online data exclusively for Rome MD Consulting PC's own internal bookkeeping. It is not offered as a service to any third party.

Data accessed: The integration reads transaction data from Rome MD Consulting PC's own Bilt credit card account and writes corresponding accounting entries (Purchase and Credit Card Credit transactions) to Rome MD Consulting PC's own QuickBooks Online company file.

Data handling: All data is processed on a private server under the sole administrator's direct control. Data is not transmitted to, shared with, or accessible by any third party other than the necessary API providers (Bilt and Intuit/QuickBooks Online) required to perform the integration's function.

What this integration does not do:

  • No analytics, telemetry, advertising, or tracking
  • No data sold or licensed to data brokers
  • No user accounts beyond the sole administrator
  • No marketing communications

Data retention: Accounting records written to QuickBooks Online are retained in accordance with Rome MD Consulting PC's normal accounting records retention. Transaction-state data on the integration server is retained for operational replay-safety and audit purposes.

For privacy-related questions, contact: rome.justin@gmail.com
